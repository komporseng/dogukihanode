Lynn Mitchell Obituary, Death – The Mandeville Family is deeply saddened to learn of the passing of our longtime friend, Lynn Mitchell. Lynn was not only a cherished friend but a pillar of our community, leaving an indelible mark on Mandeville’s architecture and cultural landscape.

Lynn’s contributions to our community’s architecture have left a lasting legacy that will be admired and appreciated for years to come. His vision and expertise have shaped the very essence of our town, enriching the lives of its residents and visitors. The buildings he designed and the spaces he created are a testament to his talent and dedication.

In addition to his architectural accomplishments, Lynn played an integral role in the revival of the Dew Drop Jazz & Social Hall. His unwavering commitment and hard work were instrumental in bringing this iconic venue back to life. Through his efforts, the Dew Drop Jazz & Social Hall once again became a vibrant hub of cultural activity, entertaining concert-goers bi-annually with beautiful music and fostering a sense of community and togetherness.

As we mourn the loss of our dear friend, our thoughts and prayers go out to Lynn’s family. We understand that his passing is not only a loss to us but a profound loss to his loved ones. Lynn’s memory will forever be a source of inspiration and a reminder of the positive impact one person can have on a community.

In this difficult time, we find solace in the memories we shared with Lynn and in the knowledge that his legacy will continue to shine brightly in the heart of Mandeville. The Mandeville Family extends its deepest condolences to Lynn’s family and loved ones, and we stand together in honoring a remarkable individual who enriched our lives and our community in countless ways.
