Biography

Pretty Lights was born in Sheridan, WY on November 25, 1981. Grammy-nominated electronic music artist and owner of the record label Pretty Lights Music. He performed at the 2010 Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival along with artists such as Tiesto. He began recording and writing hip hop music in high school.
