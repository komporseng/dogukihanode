Jerome Tang is rumored to be the head coach of the Kansas Wildcats. Despite the fact that the coach does not have a Wikipedia page, we will provide you with information about his biography here.

For a long time, Jerome has served as Baylor's assistant coach alongside head coach Scott Drew. He was hired as Drew's first staff in 2003.

Prior to that, for ten years he served as the head coach of the nationally renowned Heritage Christian Academy in Cleveland, Texas.  He was an integral part of the Baylors' rise to national prominence.

He served as an assistant coach for the first 14 seasons before being promoted to associate head coach in 2017. Tang has helped Baylor to a 14-year streak of 18+ win seasons, a 334-146 record that includes a 26-10 postseason record and nine NCAA Tournament appearances.

Baylor has also reached the Sweet 16 of the NCAA Tournament five times since 2010, with three of those appearances leading to the Elite Eight.

Tang graduated from Charter Oak State College with a Bachelor of Science degree in September 2007. 

And now, after the head coach of Kansas state took his resignation, people are expecting him to be assigned as the new head coach.
