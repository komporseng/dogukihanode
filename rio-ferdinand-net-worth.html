Rio Ferdinand | Net Worth and Income

As of 2023, Rio Ferdinand’s net worth is a whopping $75 million.

Earning From Contracts

Rio Ferdinand signed his first club contract with West Ham, where he earned a weekly salary of £21,000.

Afterward, Rio transferred to Leeds United in 2000 for $23,980,000. It was the biggest fee paid to the defender at the time.

During his time at Leeds, Ferdinand was earning a weekly salary of £30,000.

Similarly, he moved to Manchester United in 2002 for $39,960,000 and signed a five-year contract.

Moreover, he signed a new contract with Manchester United in 2008, with a weekly salary of £130,000.

Similarly, Rio had a base salary of $220,000 in 2008 and which bumped to $227,730 in 2012.

Afterward, he signed an extension contract with a base salary of $173,160.

Moreover, he also received a bonus of $399,600. Afterward, he moved to QPR in a free transfer signing a one-year contract.
