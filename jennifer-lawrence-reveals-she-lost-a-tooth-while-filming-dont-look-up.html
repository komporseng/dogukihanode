Jennifer Lawrence Reveals She Lost a Tooth While Filming 'Don't Look Up'

Publish date: 2024-07-02

Jennifer Lawrence had a tumultuous time on the set of Don’t Look Up.

Back in February, it was reported that the actress had been hit in the eye with glass during a stunt on set, causing production for the film to be halted.

At Don’t Look Up‘s premiere in New York City, Jennifer revealed that she had actually sustained another injury while filming the movie.

Click inside to read more…

She said that one of her greatest “personal” struggles during the shoot was losing a tooth.

“I lost a tooth pretty early in the filming,” Jennifer said, explaining that one of her veneers fell off. “And I couldn’t go to the dentist until the end of the movie, so I had to film most of the movie toothless.”

Her co-star Leonardo DiCaprio also discussed his decision to work on the movie and how it relates to climate change.

“We’d all been wanting to get the message out there about the climate crisis, and Adam [McKay, director] really cracked the code with creating this narrative,” he said.

Leo continued, “It’s really hard to reinvent the wheel as far as articulating the science of climate crisis, but what he did here was create a sense of urgency. We all wanted to be a part of a movie that, from an artistic standpoint, I think shifted the paradigm and made us start having conversations hopefully in a different type of way.”
