Adeline Rudolph- Biography

Adeline Rudolph is a model and actress of German-Korean descent. She rose to international prominence after starring as Agatha in the Netflix series “Chilling Adventures of Sabrina.” Adeline also appeared on “Riverdale” as Minerva Marble. She is presently in the news and gaining attention after her name was revealed to be one of the key characters in the upcoming film “Resident Evil.”

Adeline Rudolph- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Parents

Adeline Rudolph was born in Hong Kong on February 10, 1995. Caroline Rudolph, her twin sister, is also a model. Her father was German and her mother was Korean. In Hong Kong, she attended the German-Swiss International School. She attended King George V Academy, according to numerous websites, but this isn’t confirmed. She went to University College London to study Fashion Design after graduating from high school. In 2007, she received her diploma from the university. Adeline Rudolph- Professional Career

She acknowledged in an interview that she had no intention of pursuing this job or modeling and that she was just an ordinary girl. She did it for fun, modeling for her buddies. But fate intervened, and she was called by a slew of modeling agencies. She then signed with Los Angeles-based Next Model Management, Hamburg-based PMA, and Hong Kong-based Primo. On the ramp, she began to captivate passers-by. She went on to become a well-known model, appearing in editorials for magazines such as Elle, Fashion, and Marie Claire.

She has also supported several brands, including Elie Saab, Valentino, Chloe Love Story, and Guerlain. Aside from that, she has a role in the Netflix series “The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina.” She was cast in one of the principal parts, Agatha. She then landed a part as Minerva Marble in the TV show “Riverdale.” Her popularity soared as a result of both of these roles. She is now poised to play one of the key characters in the upcoming Netflix series “Resident Evil.”
