Maika Monroe is an American actress, professional kiteboarder, voice artist, and producer who first gained acclaim with her role as Jaimie “Jay” Height in the 2014 supernatural psychological horror film It Follows, a role that won her the Home Media Magazine Award for “Best Scream Queen”, the award for “Breakthrough Performer” at the Hamptons International Film Festival, and Fangoria Chainsaw Award for “Best Actress”. Over the years, she has also been cast in a variety of other films and shows which include the likes of The Bling Ring, Mark Felt: The Man Who Brought Down the White House, Labor Day, At Any Price, The Guest, Greta, Echoes of War, The 5th Wave, Brothers by Blood, Villains, Independence Day: Resurgence, Hot Summer Nights, JJ Villard’s Fairy Tales, The Scent of Rain and Lightning, Bokeh, The Stranger, I’m Not Here, The Tribes of Palos Verdes, After Everything, Tau, Honey Boy, Watcher, How To Be Alone, Flashback, Significant Other, Flying Monkeys, Burned, and Eleventh Hour.

    Maika Monroe Facts

  • Maika Monroe has an Aichmophobia, which essentially is a phobia of knives and other sharp things.
  • In 2012, she signed a lucrative deal with clothing brand, Airbound Apparel to create her own line of tank tops and t-shirts. The proceeds from the sales were donated to various local charities in the Dominican Republic.
