Who is Frank Mancuso Jr?

Frank Mancuso Jr is an American Film Producer and Writer. Frank is famous as the producer of Species (Horror Action) 1995, Species II (Horror Action) 1998 and, I Know Who Killed Me (Horror Mystery) 2007.

In 2016, he produced Johnny Frank Garrett’s Last Word (Crime drama).

Frank Mancuso Jr: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, and Education

Frank was born on 9th October 1958 in Buffalo, New York, United States. He is the son of Frank Mancuso Sr and Fay Mancuso.

His father is a former Paramount pictures president meanwhile his mother is a housewife. Besides that, Jr holds American nationality with Caucasian ethnicity.

Moving towards his educational background Frank Mancuso Jr went to Stockton University.

Frank Mancuso Jr: Career

Producer

Frank started his career in 1981 as an associate producer of Friday the 13th Part 2 (Horror Mystery).

In the following year, he produced the Friday the 13th Part III (Horror Mystery). Similarly, Jr made his executive producer in Friday the 13th Part VII: The New Blood (Horror Thriller).

Frank Mancuso Jr was cast as executive producer in War of the Worlds (TV Series) in 1990. In the following year, he produced He Said, She Said (Comedy-drama). Similarly, Mancuso was cast as executive producer in The Limbic Region (TV Movie) in 1996.

He is the executive producer of Species III (Video) in 2004. In the following year, he worked as executive producer for The Lost City (Romance drama) 2005. Moreover, Jr is the producer of I Know Who Killed Me (Horror Mystery) in 2007.

Besides that, Mancuso produced the April Fool’s Day (Video) in 2011. He was seen as an executive producer in TV Movies such as Restless and 10 years. In 2016, he produced Johnny Frank Garrett’s Last Word (Crime Drama).

Writer and actor

Frank Mancuso J is the writer of Friday the 13th: The Series (TV Series) from 1987-to 1990. Furthermore, Jr debuted as Radio Cop in Internal Affairs (Crime thriller drama) in 1990.
