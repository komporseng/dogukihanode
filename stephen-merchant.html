Biography

Stephen Merchant was born in Hanham, England on November 24, 1974. English actor, screenwriter and producer who earned eight Primetime Emmy nominations for his contributions to the popular NBC sitcom The Office. His other television writing credits include Life’s Too Short and Hello Ladies, the latter of which he also starred in. He also appeared as the character Caliban in the 2017 film Logan. He and Ricky Gervais co-starred on Extras with Ashley Jensen. He had his first entertainment job as a broadcaster on the University of Warwick’s radio station. His earliest screenwriting work includes The 11 O’Clock Show and an episode of Comedy Lab.
