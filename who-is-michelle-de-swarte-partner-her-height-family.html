Michelle de Swarte On HBO Max’s The Baby

Michelle de Swarte plays “Natasha” on HBO Max’s The Baby.

In its inaugural season, Natasha was unable to conceive a baby and frustrated by the fact that her friends were one-by-one becoming moms, she embarked on a solo getaway at a remote cabin to clear her head.

However, during her seaside trip, a mysterious baby falls off a cliff and into her arms, and she witnesses four freak-accident deaths. So, ironically, when the very thing she wanted the dearest fell into her arms, she lacked even the most basic maternal instincts.

Now, here’s the twist! No matter, how many times Natasha tries to get away from the baby (knowing that it’s evil), the infant seemingly always found its way back to her.

Talking about the series, Michelle shared that she could “quite easy to tap into what Natasha was feeling,” as she didn’t have a baby herself. Also, she shed light on the fact that working with babies can be quite cumbersome.“Babies have zero respect for being on set. So, there were a lot of tears because they’re babies and there’s a lot of stimulation and lights and sounds,” she explained.

Thus, when the baby was finally playing its part, she worked around them and made sure not to get her lines wrong. Because another retake might not be the same.

Joining Michelle this season were her co-stars, Amira Ghazalla, Amber Grappy, Shvorne Marks, and Isy Suttie.

All excited for the show’s premiere, Michelle took it to her IG to share, “To mothers and the children that made them #TheBabyHBO premieres April 24th on @hbo.”

Michelle de Swarte Net Worth

Michelle de Swarte garnered a net worth of under $700 thousand by 2022.

An actress, comedian, writer, and TV Presenter by profession, Michelle was initially spotted by a model scout at her work (a theme park called Seaworld in London) at the age of 19. She then moved to New York and started working in the comedy scene.

After working in the comedy and modeling field for years, she then returned to London and made her acting debut as a series regular in the Netflix series The Duchess (2020). Later, that year, she then signed a development deal with Various Artists Limited (for Channel 4) for her own autobiographical comedy series.

But in fact, The Duchess wasn’t her first time appearing on TV. Back in 2011, she hosted the celebrity gossip show Dirty Digest on E4, which she also co-devised. Sadly, the show was axed after only three episodes.

Talking about her modeling career, Michelle has appeared on Ready to wear – Autumn/Winter 2000, Ready to wear – Spring/Summer 2002, and Ready to wear – Autumn/Winter 2002, fashion shows. Also, she’s worked on advertisements for BCBG ‘BCBGirls’ fragrance, DKNY, Kenneth Cole fragrance, Ralph Lauren, and Tommy Jeans.

As per the actress, her favorite thing about modeling was “the money.”
