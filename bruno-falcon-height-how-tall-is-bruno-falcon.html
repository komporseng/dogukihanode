How tall is Bruno Falcon?

Publish date: 2024-05-31

Bruno ‘Pop N Taco’ Falcon, a pioneer of street dancing and the start of the 1984 smash hit Breakin’, died on Saturday at the age of 58.

Falcon was also well-known for his numerous collaborations with Michael Jackson, most notably his performance in Smooth Criminal, the centerpiece of the 1988 film Moonwalker.
